Chocolate Cupcakes with Peanut Butter Icing

Description

These are bomb AF.

Ingredients - Cake

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Cocoa
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Oil
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 Eggs (slightly beaten)
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/2 cup Hot Water

Directions - Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. GLine 18 cupcake tins.
  3. In a large bowl sift together dry ingredients.
  4. Add oil, milk, vanilla, beaten eggs and blend well. Stir in hot water and mix well.
  5. Fill lined cupcake tins. Bake for 22-25 minutes. Cool completely before icing.

Ingredients - Icing

  • 1/2 cup Butter, unsalted, softened
  • 1 Cup Peanut Butter
  • 3 Tbsp Hot Water
  • 2 cups Icing Sugar

Directions - Icing

  1. Blend together butter and peanut butter until light and fluffy. Gradually add icing sugar and hot water (alternating) until desired texture is achieved. Ice cupcakes. Enjoy!!
