Carrot Cake with Cream Cheese Icing

Description

This recipe was altered by my mom, who originally got it from a cookbook called "The Happy Baker" by Erin Bolger

My mom made some key changes! This is the recipe for the best cupcakes you'll ever try (That's a promise!!)

Ingredients - Cake

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 1 cup Vegetable Oil
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 cups Finely grated carrots
  • 1 can(398ml) Crushed pineapple, with juice
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Walnuts
  • 1/2 cup Chopped Pecans
  • 1 cup Shredded coconut (toasted)

Directions - Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Grease and flour 2 9-inch round cake pans
  3. In a large mixing bowl sift together flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and cinnamon
  4. Add sugar, oil and eggs and mix together for about 3 minutes with a hand mixer.
  5. Stir in carrots, pineapple, chopped nuts, and coconut.
  6. our into prepared pans. Bake in preheated oven for about 35 minutes or until center is set. When you insert a toothpick and it comes out clean.
  7. Remove from oven and let it cool for about 10 minutes. Then transfer to cooling racks to continue cooling.

Ingredients - Cream Cheese Icing

  • 1/2 cup Butter, unsalted, softened
  • 8 oz Package cream cheese, softenedBaking Powder
  • 2 cups Icing Sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 1/3 cup Shredded coconut, toasted

Directions - Cream Cheese Icing

  1. Cream together the butter and cream cheese.
  2. Add vanilla.
  3. Stir in the icing sugar.
  4. Spread evenly on the top of the first layer.
  5. Add the second layer and spread evenly all over the cake.
  6. Sprinkle top and sides with toastee coconut.
  7. Refrigerate.
